EduConnect User Guide

Welcome to EduConnect! This user guide will help you navigate and utilize all the features our platform offers to enhance your remote education experience.

Getting Started

Follow these steps to get started with EduConnect:

  1. Create an Account: Go to the sign-up page and register using your email address. Verify your email to activate your account.
  2. Set Up Your Profile: Complete your profile by adding your name, profile picture, and other relevant information.
  3. Explore the Dashboard: Navigate through the dashboard to familiarize yourself with the features and tools available.

Using Key Features

1. Real-Time Video Conferencing

To start a video conference:

  1. Go to the "Video Conferencing" section in the dashboard.
  2. Click on "Start a Meeting" and invite participants by sharing the meeting link.
  3. Use the available controls to manage participants, share your screen, and record the session.

2. Interactive Whiteboard

To use the interactive whiteboard:

  1. Navigate to the "Whiteboard" section in the dashboard.
  2. Click on "New Whiteboard" to start a new session.
  3. Use the drawing, text, and shape tools to create and share content in real-time.

3. Resource Sharing

To share resources:

  1. Go to the "Resources" section in the dashboard.
  2. Click on "Upload" and select the files you want to share.
  3. Organize your resources into folders and share them with specific classes or groups.

4. Assignment Submission

To submit an assignment:

  1. Navigate to the "Assignments" section in the dashboard.
  2. Select the assignment you want to submit.
  3. Upload your work and click "Submit" to send it to your instructor.
  4. Check the status of your submission and view feedback once itâ€™s graded.

5. Discussion Forums

To participate in discussion forums:

  1. Go to the "Forums" section in the dashboard.
  2. Select a forum topic to join the conversation.
  3. Post your questions, replies, and engage with other participants.

6. Instant Messaging

To use instant messaging:

  1. Click on the "Chat" icon in the dashboard.
  2. Select a contact or group to start a conversation.
  3. Send messages, share files, and stay connected with your peers.
